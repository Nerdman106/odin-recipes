Description

Pancakes are a classic breakfast dish made from a simple batter, cooked on a griddle or frying pan until golden brown. They are fluffy and delicious, often served with syrup, butter, fruits, or other toppings.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Buttermilk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Wisk the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
  2. Wisd together the buttermilk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Heat up a non-stick frying pan with butter or oil
  4. Pour 1/4 cup in the pan for each pancake.
  5. Flip pancakes and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown and cooked through.
  6. Serve arm with our favourite toppings.
