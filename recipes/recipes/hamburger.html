Description

Hamburgers are a popular and versatile dish consisting of a cooked patty of ground meat, typically beef, placed inside a sliced bun. They can be customized with a variety of toppings and condiments.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Salt and pepper
  • Hamburger buns
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Pickles
  • Mayonnaise

Instructions

  1. Preheat a grill or stovetop gril pan to medium heat.
  2. Divide the ground beef into 4 portals.
  3. Season both sides of each patty with salt and pepper.
  4. Place the patties on the grill and cook for about 3-4 minutes each side.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns.
  6. Assemble the burgers.
